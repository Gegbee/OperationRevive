Operation Revive:

> This is Your Job

Two days ago, a hard drive from the 1997 Bristol police department was found. On this hard drive were numerous unsolved case files. Your job is to use the text files in each folder to solve the cases and bring justice to the victim's family. This is Operation: Revive.

> What's next?

Click the file system to start investigating. There are four categories of evidence { POI(person of interest) notes, crime scene report, autopsy report, and POI interviews }. Use each of the pieces of evidence to write down notes and come to a conclusion. There is no reward for getting it right (yet), but just pure satisfaction and entertainment.
